# Weekly Routine

#personal #routine

## Fixed schedule (do not move)

| Day | Time | What |
|---|---|---|
| Mon, Wed, Fri | 3:30 PM | Pick up kids from school |
| Sunday | 2 PM | Family lunch |
| Tuesday | 8 AM | Stand-up with Client A |
| Thursday | 10 AM | Check-in with Client B |

## Deep work windows

- Monday / Wednesday / Friday: 10 PM – 1 AM (after the kids are asleep)
- Saturday: 9 AM – 12 PM (if partner is home)

## Rituals that work

- Review Obsidian before bed (5 min) — capture what happened during the day
- Aya briefing when waking up — do not open email before that
- No meetings before 9 AM or after 5 PM (hard limit)

## Things that drain time without value

- Real-time email monitoring (batch: 9 AM and 4 PM only)
- Meetings without a documented agenda (cancel or request agenda 24h in advance)
- Slack after 7 PM
